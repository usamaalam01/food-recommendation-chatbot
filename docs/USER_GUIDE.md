## Welcome!

The Food Recommendation Chatbot is your personal kitchen companion that helps you discover delicious recipes based on your preferences. Simply tell it what you're in the mood for, and it will suggest recipes tailored to your tastes!

---

## Getting Started

### Opening the App

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the chatbot URL (typically `http://localhost:8501`)
3. You'll see a friendly chat interface ready to help you find recipes

### Your First Conversation

Just type what you're looking for in the chat box at the bottom of the screen. Here are some examples:

- "I want something spicy for dinner"
- "Quick breakfast ideas"
- "Something with chicken that takes less than 30 minutes"
- "I'm craving Italian food"

---

## How to Use the Chatbot

### Step 1: Tell It What You Want

Start by describing what kind of food you're looking for. You can mention:

- **Meal type**: breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack, or dessert
- **Cuisine**: Indian, Italian, Mexican, Pakistani, etc.
- **Ingredients**: chicken, vegetables, rice, etc.
- **Cooking time**: quick meals, under 30 minutes, etc.
- **Flavors**: spicy, creamy, light, etc.

**Example conversation:**
```
You: I'm looking for a quick dinner
Bot: How much time do you want to spend cooking?
You: About 30 minutes
Bot: Which cuisine are you in the mood for?
You: Something Indian
Bot: Here's what I recommend: [Shows recipes]
```

### Step 2: Answer Follow-up Questions

The chatbot may ask you a few questions to better understand your preferences:

- "Is this for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or a snack?"
- "How much time do you want to spend cooking?"
- "Which cuisine are you in the mood for?"
- "Any specific ingredient or craving you have in mind?"

Simply answer in your own words - the chatbot understands natural language!

### Step 3: Browse Your Recommendations

Once the chatbot has enough information, it will show you personalized recipe recommendations. Each recipe card includes:

- **Recipe Image** - See what the dish looks like
- **Recipe Name** - The name of the dish
- **Cuisine & Course** - Type of cuisine and meal category
- **Cooking Time** - How long it takes to prepare
- **Description** - A brief summary of the dish
- **Ingredients** - What you'll need
- **Nutrition Facts** - Calories, protein, carbs, and fat
- **Why This?** - Explanation of why this recipe matches your preferences

### Step 4: See More Options

Want to see more recipes? Just ask:

- "Show me more"
- "Next"
- "Any other options?"

The chatbot will show you additional recipes that match your preferences.

Mention your dietary needs:

## Common Questions

### Q: What if no recipes match my preferences?

The chatbot will automatically try to relax some constraints to find similar recipes. If it still can't find matches, try:
- Removing some restrictions
- Being more flexible with cooking time
- Trying a different cuisine

### Q: Can I go back to previous recommendations?

Yes! Just scroll up in the chat to see your previous conversations and recommendations.

### Q: How accurate is the nutrition information?

Nutrition facts are approximate values based on standard recipe calculations. Actual values may vary based on specific ingredients and portions.

### Q: What cuisines are available?

The chatbot has recipes from various cuisines including:
- Indian
- Pakistani
- Italian
- Mexican
- And more!

### Q: Can I save my favorite recipes?

Currently, recipes are displayed in the chat. You can screenshot or note down recipes you like. The chat history is preserved during your session.

## Troubleshooting

### The chatbot isn't responding
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try again in a few moments

### Recommendations seem off
- Provide more specific preferences
- Click "Start New Recommendation" to reset
- Try describing what you want differently

### Page is loading slowly
- The first load may take a few seconds
- Subsequent interactions should be faster
